Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who we are

VellumCadence is an AI-powered content studio based in the Netherlands.
Website: vellumcadence.com
Contact: hello@vellumcadence.com

2. What data we collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business type and website
  • Your content goals and budget range

We do not collect payment data, passwords, or sensitive personal information.

3. How we use your data

We use the information you submit exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Send you the free content plan you requested
  • Follow up about our services if relevant

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data storage

Form submissions are processed via our secure API (api.vellumcadence.com) and stored in our CRM. We retain your data for up to 24 months unless you request deletion.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: hello@vellumcadence.com

6. Cookies

Our website may use Google Analytics to track anonymous visitor statistics. No personal data is collected through cookies without your consent.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version is always available at vellumcadence.com/privacy.
